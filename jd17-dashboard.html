Methodology & Field Definitions
Calls Made: Total dials per session
Answered: Any pickup including hangups
Engaged: Substantive conversation ≥30s
Satisfied: No grievance or issue-but-content
Cases: Issues logged in Fireside CRM
Answer Rate: Answered ÷ Calls Made
Satisfaction Rate: Satisfied ÷ Engaged (excludes hangups)
Hours: 6:30 PM – 8:30 PM, Mon–Thu
Staff: 1–3 agents per evening
Bilingual: Spanish agent from Dec 2024
CRM: Fireside — tagged "outreach"
List Size: ~22,000 Franklin; NB/SBB supplemental
Resolution: ~20 day avg with municipality
