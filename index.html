⚠️ Data Integrity Flags & Methodology Notes
🔶
Date Format Inconsistency
Excel serial integers, plain sequential row numbers, and text labels all appear in the DATE column. Feb 2025 North Brunswick rows use sequential integers (34–37). All dates have been normalized to YYYY-MM-DD via serial conversion (base: 1899-12-30). Verify: Feb NB rows assigned to 2025-02.
🔶
Satisfaction Definition Drift
Satisfaction metric definition evolved across the program. Nov 2024 report explicitly noted need to "discuss what actively satisfied means vs. passively satisfied." The 'x_satisfied' (extremely satisfied) column was added Dec 2024 but is sparsely populated. Month-over-month satisfaction comparisons should be treated as directional, not precise.
🟡
Duplicate Sheet — May 2025
Two "Current tracking" sheets exist in HRRRJS_working_metrics.xlsx with diverging May totals (400 vs. 231 calls). The 400-call figure aligns with the narrative report. The 231-call sheet is treated as a draft artifact and excluded from all calculations.
🟢
Cumulative Totals Cross-Verified
Monthly totals from narrative reports cross-verify with row-level spreadsheet data within acceptable variance. Nov→Dec→Jan→Feb→Mar→Apr→May chain validates. Cumulative case count of 82 (65 through May 2025 + 17 from Apr 2026) is confirmed.

Methodology & Definitions
Field Definitions
Calls Made: Total dials per session
Answered: Any pickup (including hangups)
Engaged: Substantive conversation ≥30 seconds
Satisfied: No grievance OR issue-but-content
X-Satisfied: Active supporters or enthusiastic
Cases: Issues logged in Fireside CRM
Answer Rate: Answered ÷ Calls Made
Satisfaction Rate: Satisfied ÷ Engaged
Operational Parameters
Hours: 6:30 PM – 8:30 PM, Monday–Thursday
Staff: 1–3 agents per evening

List Size: ~22,000 Franklin records; NB/SBB added
Case Resolution: ~20-day average with municipality
Satisfaction Note: Excludes hangups and out-of-district
